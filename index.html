Hello, I'm Adam Watson. I build applications for Web and Mobile.

From as far back as I can remember, I have always loved building things. I was that kid who had to pull things apart to know how they worked, much to the dismay of my parents!

In highschool woodshop, I hand built my first electric guitar after obsessively studying the work of Danny Ferrington. I'll never forget the experience of walking through the lumber yard with my dad, hand selecting the maple and alder planks that I would eventually bookmatch, hand saw, and carve into a loud and proud six string electric guitar.

Today, I get that same joy creating web and mobile experiences used by thousands of people to enhance their lives. However, instead of carving exotic hardwoods, my raw materials are Figma mocks and frameworks.
